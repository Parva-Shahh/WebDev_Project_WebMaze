About John O'Reilly - the mindmaster behing O'Reilly's Wheels

Welcome!

Thank you for visiting O'Reilly's Wheels!

My name is John O'Reilly and I am the mind behing this outstanding collection. I have always been fascinated by the car business since I was 12 when my mother and my grandfather used to bring me to the "Car Exhibitions - Auto Show" in my home town. I remember my first time in those exhibitions, when I was 7. There was a draw to win a ride in a Mercedes and my grandfather won and invited me and my mom to go with him. It was fantastic.

I have a Bachelor in Mechanical Engineering by Stanford University and a Master in Business Administration by Massachusetts Institute of Technology. I graduated in 2003 and I have 21 years of experience in the car business.

During my life, I have obtained a huge collection of unique, special and idiosyncratic collection of cars. This website has the purpose of showcase this wonderful collection and help people to find the perfect car and bring car culture to the mass public. I am here to help you!
